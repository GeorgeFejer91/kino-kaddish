Dear Dr.\ Scherbakova,

This letter serves as an official invitation to \textbf{\EventName}, which will take place on \textbf{\EventDate} at \textbf{\EventLocation}.

The event is conceived as a curated day programme with a historical guided tour, two lectures, a break with refreshments, a film screening, and a public discussion afterwards. At present, we are planning for \textbf{38 participants}; subject to approval by Museum Berlin-Karlshorst, we would like to keep open the possibility of hosting up to \textbf{56 people}.

Your contribution is currently planned as a 30-minute lecture tentatively entitled \emph{Askoldov, Censorship, and Soviet Memory Politics} from \textbf{14:00 to 14:30}, as well as your participation in the public discussion from \textbf{17:15 to 18:00}.

An honorarium of \textbf{EUR~500} is offered for your contribution.

If desired, we would be glad to arrange hotel accommodation for you for the night of \textbf{Saturday, 18 April 2026} to \textbf{Sunday, 19 April 2026}. We kindly ask you to book your train journey yourself; the costs will, of course, be reimbursed in full upon presentation of the receipts.
